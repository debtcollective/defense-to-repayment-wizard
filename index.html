Defense to Repayment App

Welcome Corinthian Collective members! We built this app to help you dispute your federal student loan debt from your school. It will guide you through a series of questions about your experience with Corinthian, and turn your answers into a form that we will submit on your behalf to the US Department of Education. You will be able to download your completed form after you submit to keep for your own records.

The personal information you provide here will only be used to fill out the Defense to Repayment form. We will not share it with anybody else without your permission.

Please answer the following questions and provide as much detail as you can in your response. The more information you can provide, the better chance you have of getting a loan discharge. Filling out the form will probably take around 30 minutes. If a question doesn’t apply to you, you can leave it blank and click Next.
